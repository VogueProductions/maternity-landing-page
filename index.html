Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best month for a maternity photoshoot?

The ideal time is between the 7th and 8th month of pregnancy when the baby bump is beautifully visible while you are still comfortable enough to pose.

Where is Vogue Productions located in Agra?

Our Agra studio is at 12, Vaibhav Gardens Phase 2, 100-ft Road, Dayal Bagh, Agra 282005. We also have studios in Lucknow (Gomti Nagar) and Kanpur (Shyam Nagar).

Do you provide gowns for the maternity shoot?

Yes. Every Silver, Gold and Platinum package includes designer gowns from our wardrobe — from boho to royal couture. Add-on dress changes are ₹1,000 each.

How much does a maternity shoot cost in Agra?

Our maternity packages start at ₹4,999 (Bronze) and go up to ₹14,999 (Platinum) which includes outdoor + indoor locations, gowns, hard-bind album and a cinematic reel.

How quickly will I receive the photos?

RAW images are shared the next day after your shoot. The fully edited gallery and any reels are delivered within 7 days.
